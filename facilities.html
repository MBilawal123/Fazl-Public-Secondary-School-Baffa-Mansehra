Facilities For Student's

Medical

Health is the greatest blessing of God. For the sound health of the students the school and college have their own infirmaries i.e. small hospital. Nursing staff looks after the sick children, while a part time doctor visits regularly. Serious cases are referred to specialists and hospitals. The medical bills are paid by school but charged from parents with the quarterly fee.

Books and Stationary

The school and college have their own well-stocked books and stationery stores. Students are not allowed to use exercise books other than those specially printed for the institution. The school/college ensures that prices are not higher than the market

Computer Literacy

To equip students with modern technology, the institution has a well-equipped computer lab for boarders and day scholars. Computer Science is included as a subject for Matric & F.Sc, along with optional training for junior and senior classes. Internet facilities also exist in both institutions.

Security System

The college and school premise is fortified with strong walls further secured with barbed wire and being secured by huge number of armed security guards, well trained in their trade craft to protect the human assets of the Institution. The school and college premises inside & outside including parking, grounds, classrooms, administrative & academics blocks etc all are secured with number of security cameras.

Class Rooms

The college has sufficient number of classrooms, well spacious and well-furnished to facilitate the students to get education in a suitable and comfortable atmosphere.

The Student's Mess

College has separate mess for boys and girls, the menu is set according to the dietary requirements of the students. The mess is being run by a mess committee headed by PMC and assisted by a mess secretary.

WASHROOM

A sufficient number of toilets are available to the students built on standard scale.

Cleanliness

A huge fleet of sanitary staff remains on toe to provide filth free clean and healthy environment in the college.
